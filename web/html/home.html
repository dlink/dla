David Link is an award winning Hudson Valley artist, and an abstract minimalist artists who's work consists of clean geometric forms, positive and negative shapes that have balance and harmony. He's art work includes sculptures, paintings, and drawings. He is in many private collections around the United States and shows in many Hudson Valley galleries.

Art is an expression of ourselves. It is a way to communicate what we feel and what we see to the outside world. It is a way of sharing our unique human experiences.

My art is about form and space. It is an interplay of angles, edges, and surfaces in three-dimensional space and the negative space it defines. I work with geometric mathematical forms that are both minimal and bold in the hope of conveying simple harmony and elegance in design.

- David Link
